Who We Are

RCCG Middle East Region 2

The Redeemed Christian Church Of God


From a humble beginning in South-Western Region of Nigeria, The Redeemed Christian Church Of God(The RCCG), has over the years, grown spiritually and numerically, and has also spread all over the continents of the world. Thus, it has assumed a global spiritual movement for world evangelization, revival and soul winning with the aim of expanding the kingdom of God on the earth, while awaiting the return of the Lord Jesus Christ. The Church has witnessed a great deal of structural and administrative re-engineering, since 1981 when Pastor E. A. Adeboye emerged the General Overseer of the Mission after the exit of the Pioneer Founder and General Superintendent, Pastor Josiah Akindayomi.

Our Vision


The vision for the Redeemed Christian Church of God Worldwide are:

To make heaven.

To take as many people with us.

To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations.

To accomplish No. 1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle.

To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within fifteen minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.

We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ.



Our Mission

RCCG Mission Worldwide

Mission is an expression of how we shall get the vision accomplished. It is the strategies to employ to fulfil the vision. These are that:

Holiness will be our lifestyle.

We will plant churches within 5 minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and within five minutes' drive distance in every city and town of developed countries.
