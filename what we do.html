Organs Of The Church


Upon assumption of office by Pastor E. A. Adeboye as the General Overseer (G.O.) of the RCCG in 1981 and in his determined efforts to re-engineer the church to achieve its global vision and mandate through aggressive evangelization, teaching and discipleship, the following organs were institutionalized in the church.



BELIEVERS CLASS

The Believers Class Program is a training program every growth-thirsty Christian should endeavour to attend. It seekss to create in every believer a foundation which is solid enough to withstand any type of structure that would thereafter come upon it; a foundation firm enough to hold in place the whole structure against both gentle and boisterous winds of life which blow naturally upon every building. -Matt. 7:25.



SCHOOL OF DISCIPLES

History Of The School Of Disciples

The School Of Disciples was established in 1985 through Christ The Redeemer's Ministers, an evangelistic arm of the Redeemed Christian Church Of God. The School was founded by the overseer of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, Pastor E. A. Adebayo, who is also the principal of the School presently, under the leadership of the Vice President (Education), Pastor (Mrs.) Folu Adeboye.



HOUSE FELLOWSHIP

Every parishioner is encouraged to belong to the House Fellowship Center closest to him. The House Fellowship program of the Church is managed and coordinated by the House Fellowship Directorate. A parishioner is regarded a true member of the Church only when he become a committed and participating member of a House Fellowship. This is why every Parish of the Mission must give a priority attention to House Fellowship and ensure that every Church attendee becomes a House Fellowship Member.
