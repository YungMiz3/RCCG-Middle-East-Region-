Pastor (Mrs) Adeola Mensah


Pastor (Mrs) Adeola Mensah, is an ordained minister in the Redeemed Christian Church of God. She was the Pastor in charge of RCCG Lagos Province 15, Nigeria and now she Oversee the RCCG Middle East Region 2; she also doubles up as the General Overseer of the REdeemed Christian Church of God.

She serves on the Board of the Central Mission Board (CMB) of the Redeemed Christian Church of God.

She is happily married with children.
